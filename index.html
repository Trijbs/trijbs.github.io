Who is Trijbs?

A creator from the Netherlands who refused to pick a lane. Design, code, skate culture, music — it all started in the same place: needing to make something and figuring out how.

Trijbs is the name for everything that happens when visuals, technology, and street culture stop being separate categories and start colliding.
